# Tech Stack Mapper Skill

**Purpose**: Generate contextualized technology recommendations based on organizational constraints, team capabilities, and project requirements. Does NOT prescribe universal "best" tools - instead provides decision framework that accounts for human and political realities of technology selection.

**Key Insight**: Technology choices are rarely pure technical decisions. They're constrained by team skills, budget realities, political dynamics, risk tolerance, existing investments, and time pressure. This skill makes those constraints explicit and generates recommendations that fit YOUR reality.

---

## Invocation

User invokes this skill by asking: "Help me choose technology for [capability]"

The skill runs a two-phase process:
1. **Phase 1: Discovery** - Ask questions to understand organizational constraints
2. **Phase 2: Recommendation** - Generate contextualized technology recommendations
